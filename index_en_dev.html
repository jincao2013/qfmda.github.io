Welcome to Yao Group

Quantum functional material design and application lab is affiliated with School of Physics, Beijing Institute of Technology. The lab is supervised by Prof. Yugui Yao, who is a Cheung Kong Scholars Chair Professor and Distinguished Young Scholar in China. Lab members include 4 associate professors, 2 lecturers, 3 postdoctors, ~10 graduate students. Our main research areas focus on first-principles calculations in spintronics (especially electronic structures of spin-orbital coupling system), exotic quantum property and novel energy materials...
