Research

  1. Search for new two/three-dimensional topological insulators with excellent physical properties, e.g., large bulk band gap and easy growth or fabrication in experiments.
  2. Explore new topological phases in real materials, such as three-dimensional Dirac and Weyl semimetals, and study the anomalous and spin Hall effects in them.
  3. Study novel magneto-optical phenomenon and nonlinear optical property in low-dimensional materials with strong spin-orbit coupling.
