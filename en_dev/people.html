Research

In the field of theoretical condensed-matter physics and computational physics, I investigate the novel electric, optic, and magnetic properties of condensed-matter materials using first-principles calculations, tight-bind model, Monte Carlo method, analytical derivation, and so on. At present, my research mainly concentrates on various interesting phenomena and their theories in low-dimensional condensed-matter systems.
